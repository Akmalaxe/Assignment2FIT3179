Global Economic and Human Development Overview

Global GDP Distribution

China was the only major economy to grow in 2020.

Brazil's economy heavily relies on commodity exports like soybeans.

India has one of the world's fastest growing major economies.

The United States has the world's largest economy by GDP.

Human Development Comparison Analysis

Countries with higher GDP often record stronger HDI performance overall. Norway and the United States show both wealth and human progress. India and Nigeria illustrate slower GDP growth and HDI improvement. Regional differences highlight how income directly supports social development. Stronger economies invest more in education, healthcare, and living standards.
